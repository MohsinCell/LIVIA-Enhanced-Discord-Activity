Features

Everything you need to share your music

Live Lyrics

Real-time synced lyrics that scroll as you listen. Your friends can follow along.

Album Art

High-resolution artwork displayed on Discord and your personal session page.

Shareable Link

Get a unique session link anyone can visit to see what you're playing live.

Privacy First

Only shares what you're currently playing. No tracking, no accounts required.

Auto-Start

Set it once and forget it. Runs silently in your system tray.

Cross-Platform

Works on Windows and macOS with any music app that uses system media controls.

Supported Apps

Automatically detects music from these apps and more

Spotify
Apple Music

How it works
